About the Owner

Mouhamed Kandil — Known to Many as Max

Founder & driving force behind Stocklot Warehouse.

Mouhamed Kandil, better known to many as Max, is the founder and driving force behind Stocklot Warehouse. He is a global businessman, international trader, negotiator, and relationship builder with years of experience in stocklots, closeouts, OEM automotive materials, PVC products, textiles, flooring, industrial materials, and global sourcing.

But more than anything, Max is a people person. He has traveled the world, worked across cultures, and built relationships across continents — understanding that international trade is not only about products and prices, but about trust, timing, communication, and connecting the right buyer with the right opportunity.

A Worldly Approach to Global Trade

Max’s experience reaches across North America, Canada, Egypt, the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and Latin America. Through this global exposure, he has developed a rare understanding of different markets, cultures, buyers, suppliers, and business expectations.

He understands how people buy.
He understands how factories sell.
He understands how containers move.
He understands how customs, documents, ports, warehouses, and timing can make or break a deal.

That is why Stocklot Warehouse was built to be more than a product listing website. It was built as a global sourcing platform for serious buyers who need value, volume, speed, and support.

Turning Stocklots Into Opportunity

Max believes that stocklots are not simply leftover inventory — they are opportunity. A smart buyer can use closeouts, surplus, factory seconds, and obsolete OEM materials to lower costs, increase margins, enter new markets, and compete more aggressively.

Whether the product is automotive leather, PVC material, marine vinyl, awning fabric, coated fabric, flooring, release paper, headliner, automotive carpet, mesh, or industrial surplus, Max’s strength is identifying where real value exists — and helping customers move that value into their market.

Built on Relationships, Speed, and Trust

Max’s reputation has been built through direct relationships with suppliers, warehouse operators, manufacturers, freight partners, customs contacts, and international buyers. His approach is simple:

Be directMove fastSolve problemsRespect peopleDeliver value

That mindset is the foundation of Stocklot Warehouse.

The Next Vision

A One-Stop Global Container Hub in Egypt

Max’s next goal is to create a powerful one-stop hub in Egypt where buyers from around the world can build their own mixed containers from multiple product categories in one location. Instead of buying from several countries, waiting on different suppliers, and managing multiple shipments, buyers will be able to use Egypt as a centralized sourcing point.

Come to Egypt. Select your products. Build your container. Ship efficiently.

This hub is designed to help global buyers combine different materials into one container — automotive materials, PVC products, textiles, flooring, marine vinyls, coated fabrics, upholstery materials, and other stocklot opportunities.

Why Egypt?

Egypt offers a unique advantage for international buyers. It is strategically located between Africa, the Middle East, Europe, and major global shipping routes — easier to travel to for many international buyers, more accessible than distant sourcing markets, and positioned as a practical trade bridge between continents. Through Egypt, buyers may benefit from:

Shorter Travel Routes
