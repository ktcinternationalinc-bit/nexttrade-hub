Know What You're Buying

Material grades, defined.

No guesswork. Here is exactly what our grading terms mean so you can specify with confidence.

Grade A

Near-prime finish. Minimal-to-no visible variation — suitable for visible, premium applications.

Grade B

Minor aesthetic variation. Fully functional material suitable for industrial use, reinforcement, and cost-sensitive production.

First-Quality Obsolete

Prime material from discontinued OEM colors/grains — full quality, simply no longer in active OEM production.

OEM Grain

Surface texture matching original-equipment automotive specifications, selectable by OEM grain code or generic description.

Trilaminate

Three-layer bonded PVC construction used for pool liners and waterproof applications — strength and water resistance in one sheet.

Bonded / Duty-Deferred

Held in a customs-bonded facility: import duty is suspended until the goods leave the warehouse, improving your cash flow.
