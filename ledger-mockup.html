Open Accounts — Running Credit Model Mockup

How it works: Every account has two running credits per currency — one tracking what each side owes the other.
• Our credit with them goes UP when we pay them, DOWN when they invoice us.
• Their credit with us goes UP when they pay us, DOWN when we invoice them.
• If a credit goes negative, that's the amount owed.
